The three collectives

OpDoesUsed for
broadcastone rank → identical copy on allsetup (share weights); TSP weight-shard circulation
all_reducesum same-shaped tensors → result on allTP output (combine row-parallel partials)
all_gatherconcatenate distinct chunks → full on allSP & TSP K/V gather (rebuild full sequence)
The rule

all_reduce sums overlapping values; all_gather concatenates distinct pieces. Every rank must call every collective in the same order or the group deadlocks.

TSP attention algorithm (memorize this)

Y_p = 0
for r in 0..D-1:
    broadcast [Wq_r, Wk_r, Wv_r, Wo_r]   # don't forget Wo!
    Q_rp = X_p @ Wq_r.T
    K_rp = X_p @ Wk_r.T
    V_rp = X_p @ Wv_r.T
    K_r, V_r = all_gather(K_rp, V_rp)     # over sequence ranks p
    A_rp = causal_attn(Q_rp, K_r, V_r)    # V, not K!
    Y_p += A_rp @ Wo_r.T                   # local sum over r; NO all_reduce
